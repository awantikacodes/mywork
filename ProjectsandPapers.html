StudentCentral

StudentCentral is a comprehensive mobile and web application designed to assist students in managing their academic and personal life more effectively. The platform offers guidance on study plans, career counseling, mental health resources, and peer networking.

The app integrates personalized advice based on user profiles, enabling students to receive tailored recommendations for courses, internships, and extracurricular activities. With real-time notifications and a community forum, StudentCentral fosters an interactive environment to support student growth.

Technologies used include React for the frontend, Node.js for backend APIs, and MongoDB for the database. The project emphasizes usability, security, and scalability to accommodate a large user base.

DigiLab

DigiLab is an innovative chemistry lab environment designed specifically for 8th to 10th standard students. It provides a virtual platform to conduct contactless experiments, simulating real lab procedures through interactive modules.

The application emphasizes step-by-step guidance, enabling students to understand chemical reactions, safety protocols, and experiment results visually. This is ideal for remote education or supplementing physical labs.

Built using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript with animations, DigiLab aims to make learning chemistry engaging and accessible.

Radius-Oriented Garbage Collector for Lakes

This motorized garbage collector features a rotating arm mechanism designed to collect floating trash from lakes efficiently. The device is powered by solar energy and includes sensors to navigate around obstacles and debris.

It aims to promote environmental sustainability by reducing pollution in water bodies and supporting local wildlife. The system includes IoT integration for remote monitoring and control.

Components used include Arduino microcontrollers, solar panels, servo motors, and water-resistant casing.

Weight-Sensitive Barrier

This project developed a weight-sensitive safety barrier designed to protect wildlife in sanctuaries by preventing animals from accidentally falling off cliffs or dangerous edges.

The barrier uses pressure sensors that detect the presence of animals and trigger alerts or activate deterrents to keep them safe.

The system focuses on durability, low maintenance, and minimal disturbance to natural habitats.

Radio Frequency Reducer

The Radio Frequency Reducer is a specialized device aimed at reducing the harmful impact of mobile device frequencies on birds within wildlife sanctuaries.

It functions by emitting counter frequencies that neutralize harmful waves, thus protecting avian species from disorientation and health hazards.

The project involves RF engineering, environmental biology collaboration, and sustainable power solutions.

Cypher - Hardware Enabled Root of Trust

Cypher is a security system designed for the UIDAI portal to protect Aadhaar photographs by establishing a hardware-based root of trust.

It ensures that images are securely stored and transmitted, preventing tampering and unauthorized access through hardware-level encryption and authentication.

The project uses TPM (Trusted Platform Module) technology, cryptographic protocols, and secure boot mechanisms.

QuantaOne

QuantaOne is a medical rover designed to autonomously navigate hospital wards and record vital patient information such as temperature, pulse, and oxygen levels.

The rover is equipped with sensors and cameras, transmitting data securely to medical professionals to improve patient monitoring efficiency and reduce human error.

It combines robotics, IoT, and healthcare technologies for practical application in medical facilities.

LegalDocX

LegalDocX is a secure document management website that allows users to store important legal documents and control access permissions for third parties.

It features encryption, audit trails, and a permission-based sharing system to ensure document confidentiality and integrity.

Developed using Django and PostgreSQL with a focus on privacy and user control.

BoardEase - Railway Modernization

BoardEase is a smart railway boarding and station modernization system aimed at improving passenger experience, safety, and operational efficiency.

It incorporates AI-driven sensors, mobile apps for passenger information, and automated safety barriers to streamline railway station processes.

The project integrates IoT devices and cloud computing for real-time data monitoring and management.
